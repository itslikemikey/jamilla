Jamilla works at the meeting point of built form and street life. Architecture is approached as something organic — structure, light, wood and landscape belonging to one another, the way a capiz window turns hard sun into something soft enough to live with.

Everything is shot on a single fixed lens. Constraint sharpens the eye.
